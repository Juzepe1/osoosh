Product Warranty Registration and Website Claim Management and Backend Claim for Enterprise Edition

Product Warranty Registration and Website Claim Management and Backend

Features:

- Allow you to configure warranty on any product in Odoo.

- System will create warranty registration automatically when you confirm Sales order and if system found warranty link with that product.

- System will set warranty registrations or warranty cards on subscription/contract related to that sales order.

- Allow your customers to claim request directly from website my account.

- Allow your customers to view all warranty in website my account portal.

- Management of claim or ticket in backend by support/claim management team.

- Print warrnty cards and send to your customer.

- Also allow you to create warranty registration manually.

- Complete management of claim request using enterprise helpdesk feature as base.

- For more details please watch video.

- Only for odoo Enterprise Edition.
For community edition of Odoo you can look and buy https://www.odoo.com/apps/modules/11.0/odoo_product_warranty_claim/
